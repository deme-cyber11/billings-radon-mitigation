Who We Are

Rim Rock Radon was founded with a single mission: to protect Montana families from the invisible danger of radon gas. Radon is the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States, and Yellowstone County consistently tests above the EPA action level of 4 pCi/L. We saw the need for a dedicated, reliable radon mitigation company that Billings homeowners could trust.

From the start, we committed to doing things right. Every system we install follows EPA guidelines and industry best practices. We use only proven sub-slab depressurization technology and back every installation with a performance guarantee. Our team is fully licensed and insured, and we carry the training and certifications needed to handle any residential radon challenge.

Today, we serve all of Yellowstone County and surrounding communities including Laurel, Lockwood, Shepherd, Park City, Columbus, Red Lodge, Hardin, and Roundup. We have helped hundreds of homeowners reduce their radon levels and breathe easier in their own homes.

Our Commitment to Billings Homeowners

At Rim Rock Radon, we take radon seriously because the stakes are real. Radon is a naturally occurring radioactive gas that seeps into homes through foundation cracks, sump pits, and construction joints. It's odorless, colorless, and invisible — but long-term exposure is the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States, behind only smoking. Montana has some of the highest average radon levels in the country, and Yellowstone County is no exception.

What sets Rim Rock Radon apart is our commitment to doing the job properly every time. We don't cut corners on system design, we don't use undersized fans to save money, and we don't declare a job finished until post-mitigation testing confirms results below the EPA action level of 4 pCi/L. If a system we install doesn't achieve that standard, we come back and make it right at no additional charge — no arguments, no fine print.

We've served hundreds of Billings-area families and understand the local geology, the typical foundation types, and the radon entry patterns common in Yellowstone County homes. That experience makes us faster, more efficient, and more reliable than a generalist contractor who handles radon as a side service. When you hire Rim Rock Radon, you're working with specialists whose entire focus is protecting your indoor air quality.
