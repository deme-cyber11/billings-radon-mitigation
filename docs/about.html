About Billings Radon Mitigation

Licensed and insured radon mitigation professionals protecting Billings families since day one.

Who We Are

Billings Radon Mitigation was founded with a single mission: to protect Montana families from the invisible danger of radon gas. Radon is the second leading cause of lung cancer in the United States, and Yellowstone County consistently tests above the EPA action level of 4 pCi/L. We saw the need for a dedicated, reliable radon mitigation company that Billings homeowners could trust.

From the start, we committed to doing things right. Every system we install follows EPA guidelines and industry best practices. We use only proven sub-slab depressurization technology and back every installation with a performance guarantee. Our team is fully licensed and insured, and we carry the training and certifications needed to handle any residential radon challenge.

Today, we serve all of Yellowstone County and surrounding communities including Laurel, Lockwood, Shepherd, Park City, Columbus, Red Lodge, Hardin, and Roundup. We have helped hundreds of homeowners reduce their radon levels and breathe easier in their own homes.
