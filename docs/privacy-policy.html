Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Billings Radon Mitigation collects information you voluntarily provide when you use our contact form, call our office, or request a service estimate. This includes your name, phone number, email address, and property details. We also collect website usage data automatically through analytics tools, including pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and general geographic location.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and service requests
  • Provide radon testing and mitigation services
  • Schedule appointments and follow up on estimates
  • Improve our website performance and user experience
  • Communicate service updates and relevant information

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Our website uses Google Analytics (GA4) to track website traffic and user behavior, and Microsoft Clarity for session recording and heatmap analysis. These tools use cookies and similar technologies to collect anonymized usage data. You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling them may affect website functionality.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While we take appropriate precautions, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Your Rights
