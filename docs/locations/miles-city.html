Radon Mitigation Services in Miles City, Montana

Miles City is the county seat of Custer County, located in eastern Montana at the confluence of the Tongue and Yellowstone rivers. With a population of approximately 8,500, Miles City is the largest community between Billings and the North Dakota border, serving as a regional hub for ranching, agriculture, and commerce in eastern Montana. The city is known for its annual Bucking Horse Sale, historic Main Street, and resilient community spirit. While Miles City sits roughly 145 miles east of Billings, it falls within our extended service area, and we are committed to providing the same professional radon mitigation services to Miles City homeowners that we offer closer to home.

Eastern Montana's geology presents radon risks that many homeowners may not be aware of. The sedimentary formations underlying Custer County, including layers of shale, sandstone, and lignite deposits, contain naturally occurring radioactive elements that produce radon gas. The EPA classifies this region as having moderate to high radon potential, and independent testing has shown that many homes in Miles City test at or above the 4 pCi/L action level. Because Miles City does not have local radon mitigation specialists, homeowners have historically had limited options for addressing this invisible health threat.

Billings Radon Mitigation is proud to extend our services to Miles City and the surrounding Custer County area. We schedule regular service trips to eastern Montana and can coordinate testing, installation, and follow-up visits efficiently. Our process is the same regardless of location: we begin with a professional radon test, design a customized mitigation system based on your home's foundation and construction, install the system in a single day, and return for post-mitigation testing to verify results. Most homes receive a sub-slab depressurization system that draws radon from beneath the foundation and vents it safely above the roofline.

Miles City homeowners choose Billings Radon Mitigation because we eliminate the challenge of finding qualified radon professionals in eastern Montana. We bring fully licensed and insured technicians, professional-grade equipment, and a performance guarantee to every Miles City job. Our free estimates and transparent pricing mean no surprises, and our guaranteed results mean your family is protected. If you are in Miles City or anywhere in Custer County and need radon testing or mitigation, we are ready to help.
