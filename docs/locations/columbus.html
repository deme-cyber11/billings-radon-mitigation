Radon Mitigation Services in Columbus, Montana

Columbus is the county seat of Stillwater County, located approximately 40 minutes west of Billings along Interstate 90. Situated at the confluence of the Stillwater River and the Yellowstone River, Columbus serves as the gateway to the Beartooth Highway and the spectacular mountain country to the south. The town has a population of around 2,000 residents and is known for its friendly community atmosphere, historic downtown, and proximity to world-class outdoor recreation.

The geological conditions in the Columbus area are typical of south-central Montana, where uranium-bearing rock formations produce radon gas as a natural byproduct of radioactive decay. This gas rises through the soil and can accumulate to dangerous concentrations inside homes if there are pathways through the foundation. The EPA classifies Stillwater County as a region with significant radon potential, making professional testing essential for every Columbus homeowner.

Radon Risks in the Stillwater Valley

Columbus sits in the Stillwater Valley, where alluvial soils deposited by river systems over thousands of years can contain elevated concentrations of naturally occurring radioactive materials. Homes built along the river valley and on the surrounding benchlands may experience different radon levels depending on their specific location, foundation type, and soil composition. The only way to determine your Columbus home's radon concentration is through professional testing.

Many homes in Columbus were built decades ago, when radon-resistant construction techniques were not yet standard practice. These older properties may have foundations with cracks, gaps around utility penetrations, and deteriorating mortar joints that provide easy entry points for radon gas. Basements and lower-level living spaces are at the highest risk because they are in direct contact with the soil. Even newer homes in the area can develop radon issues, particularly if they were built without passive radon prevention features.

Our Approach to Radon Mitigation in Columbus

Billings Radon Mitigation provides full-service radon solutions for Columbus homeowners. Our licensed and insured technicians travel to Columbus regularly, and we offer the same professional-grade service that residents of the greater Billings area have come to rely on. We begin with comprehensive radon testing to establish your home's baseline levels, then design a mitigation system customized to your specific foundation and soil conditions.

For most Columbus homes, we recommend active sub-slab depressurization, the EPA's preferred method for reducing indoor radon. This system involves drilling a suction point through the foundation slab, installing a sealed PVC vent pipe, and connecting it to a high-efficiency inline radon fan. The fan operates continuously, drawing radon from beneath the foundation and exhausting it above the roofline where it dissipates safely into the outdoor air. The system is quiet, energy-efficient, and requires very little ongoing maintenance.

Every Columbus installation includes post-mitigation testing to verify that radon levels have been successfully reduced below the EPA action level of 4 pCi/L. We stand behind our work with a written performance guarantee. If your radon levels do not drop below the action level, we return to make adjustments at no additional charge.
